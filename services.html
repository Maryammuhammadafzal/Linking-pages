ICC CRICKET

The 2023 Cricket World Cup, officially known as the 2023 ICC Men's Cricket World Cup, was the 13th edition of the Cricket World Cup. It started on 5 October and concluded on 19 November 2023, with Australia winning the tournament.ICC CRICKET It was the first men's Cricket World Cup which India hosted solely. The tournament took place in ten different stadiums, in ten cities across the country. In the first semi-final India beat New Zealand, and in the second semi-final Australia beat South Africa. The final took place between India and Australia at Narendra Modi Stadium on 19 November with Australia winning the title for the sixth time.

SHOAIB AKHTAR

The Pakistani bowler from Rawalpindi is popularly called 'The Rawalpindi Express.' Shoaib Akhtar has been one of the best bowlers coming out of Pakistan.When Shoaib Akhtar burst onto the scene, the cricketing world had never witnessed such a raw pace. It took him little time to become one of the most popular cricketers in the world, thanks to his fast and deceiving delivery and wicket-taking ability. His 161 Kmph delivery still ranks the fastest delivery ever. But that's not all; all the entries in the most immediate delivery up to number 7 belong to Shoaib Akhtar.

SHAHID AFRIDI

Shahid Afridi is one of the most aggressive batters. In our list of the most popular cricketer in the world, the Pakistani cricketer has a special place. He once held the record for the fastest century scored in an ODI at just 37 balls. Later, South Africa's AB de Villers broke his record, scoring a century in 31 balls. Afridi is popularly known as Boom Boom Afridi worldwide. His popularity is not limited to Pakistan, and he is one of the most popular cricketers in the world.

CHRIS GAYLE

Christopher Henry Gayle, a.k.a. Chris Gayle, is arguably the most popular cricket player to have ever played the game. His extraordinary nature and smashing batting have taken the world by storm, and Gayle's devastating knocks have propelled him to a multitude of records which seem impossible to break. The West Indies opener has hit two triple centuries in tests, but most importantly, his 175 of 66 in a T20 inning is the most talked-about innings of all time. This world famous batsman has a record in all three formats having also won two T20I World Cups with West Indies.

VIRAT KOHLI

Kohli is currently the world's most famous cricketer, and his fame is not limited to cricket. Famous footballers, actors, and athletes are fans of his playing style. He is the second-leading centurion ODIs after Sachin Tendulkar. He has won the Under-19 Cricket World Cup in 2008 and the ICC World Cup in 2011. How could we leave Virat Kohli from the list of the most popular cricketer in the world? He is a world famous batsman who has earned a reputation for being the 'Best Chaser'.

MS Dhoni is among the most popular cricketer in the world due to his overall team play and individual hitting prowess.
